Our Services

Vehicle Leasing Assistance

At LankaDrive Auto Sales, we make vehicle ownership easier by helping customers explore suitable leasing and financing options in a simple and stress-free way. Our experienced team guides each customer through the process step by step, clearly explaining payment plans, lease periods, required documents, and affordable solutions that match different budgets and lifestyles. We understand that choosing the right financial option can feel confusing, especially for first-time buyers, so we take the time to answer questions and provide helpful guidance. This service is designed to save time, reduce uncertainty, and make the journey toward owning your ideal vehicle more convenient, practical, and accessible.

Insurance Arrangements

We help customers arrange reliable vehicle insurance through trusted providers so they can drive with confidence from the very first day of ownership. Our staff can explain the main types of coverage, assist with the required details, and guide customers toward insurance options that offer both strong protection and good value for money. Instead of dealing with the process alone, buyers can receive support in understanding what is suitable for their needs and vehicle type. This service adds greater convenience to the purchasing experience, reduces unnecessary hassle, and gives customers peace of mind knowing their investment is protected with a dependable insurance plan.

Test Drive Booking

Choosing the right vehicle becomes much easier when you have the chance to experience it in person, which is why we offer a simple and convenient test drive booking service. Customers can schedule a suitable date and time to inspect and drive their preferred vehicle before making a final decision. A test drive allows buyers to evaluate important factors such as comfort, driving position, handling, visibility, engine response, and overall road performance in a practical setting. This service helps customers feel more certain about their choice, making the buying process more informed, realistic, and satisfying. It is an important step for anyone who wants confidence before purchasing.

Vehicle Registration Services

Our vehicle registration support service helps customers complete important documentation smoothly and without unnecessary stress. We guide buyers through the essential paperwork, explain the required steps, and help them understand the process needed for legal registration. For many customers, registration can feel time-consuming and complicated, especially when dealing with forms, approvals, and official procedures. That is why our team is ready to provide clear support and practical assistance throughout the process. This service makes the overall vehicle purchasing experience faster, easier, and more convenient, allowing customers to focus on enjoying their new vehicle rather than worrying about the paperwork involved.

Trade-In Options

Customers who already own a vehicle can benefit from our trade-in service, which makes upgrading to a newer or more suitable vehicle much more convenient. Instead of going through the trouble of advertising and selling the old vehicle separately, customers can bring it to us for evaluation as part of the purchasing process. We assess its current condition, model, and market value, then provide a fair trade-in offer that can reduce the cost of the next vehicle. This service saves valuable time, lowers effort, and makes upgrading more practical for busy customers. It also provides a smoother transition from an old vehicle to a newer one with less hassle.

Vehicle Inspection Reports

We provide vehicle inspection support to help customers buy with greater confidence, trust, and transparency. Inspection reports give useful information about the vehicle’s condition, helping buyers understand important areas such as exterior quality, interior condition, mechanical performance, and overall reliability. This is especially valuable for customers who want extra assurance before completing their purchase, as it allows them to make a more informed decision. By offering clear inspection details, we aim to increase confidence and reduce uncertainty during the buying process. This service reflects our commitment to honesty, customer satisfaction, and helping every buyer choose a vehicle that truly meets their expectations.
